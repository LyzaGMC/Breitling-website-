THE VERSATILE WATCH FROM BREITLING ADAPTED TO ALL YOUR DESIRES, AT ITS PLACE ON THE RED CARPET OR ON THE BEACH. LAUNCHED IN 1984, THE CHRONOMAT BEAUTIFULLY CELEBRATED ONE HUNDRED YEARS OF BREITLING AND MARKED THE RETURN OF THE MECHANICAL CHRONOGRAPH. NEARLY 40 YEARS LATER, THE CHRONOMAT IS STILL SYNONYMOUS WITH AMBITION, ACTION AND STYLE.
The Breitling all-star squad is made up of four sporting superstars who represent a new level of excellence in their respective disciplines. As new Chronomat ambassadors, GiannisAntetokounmpo (basketball), ErlingHaaland (football), ChloeKim (snowboarding) and TrevorLawrence (American football) perfectly illustrate the expression “always be on the move”. Whether training for a competition at home or competing to win around the world, they need a watch that goes with them everywhere, while providing them with an anchor in their country. The all-star squad's campaign takes us into the heart of the moment they spent their careers training for (the goal, the dunk, the jump, the pass), as if we were there.
